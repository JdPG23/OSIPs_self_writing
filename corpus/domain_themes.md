## Patterns in Successful OSIP Titles

1. **Memorable acronyms**: PEACE, OrbitGPT, EcoSTAR, CleanCube, LATTICE, MorphoComp4D, AirWheel, SpaceHopper
2. **Specific technology + application**: "Water Electrolysis Hall Effect Thruster", not "Novel Propulsion Concept"
3. **Action-oriented**: "Bringing change detection on board satellites", "Enabling analysis of extremely large data sets"
4. **Problem-first**: "Protecting Earth from the uncertainty of space debris re-entry (PEACE)"
5. **Quantified where possible**: "operation over -100°C to 250°C and 1Mrad TID"
