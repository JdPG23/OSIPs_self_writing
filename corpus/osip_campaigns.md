## Evaluation Process

- **Primary criterion at screening**: Innovation potential
- Evaluations by ESA domain expert panel, typically 3rd Friday of each month
- Full evaluation: project completion capacity, technical quality, programmatic suitability, risk identification, realistic budgeting
