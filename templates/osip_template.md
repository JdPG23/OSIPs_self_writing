## Title

[ACRONYM] — Full Title Describing the Innovation (max 15 words)

## Problem Statement

[2-3 paragraphs]
- What is the specific technical problem or capability gap?
- Why does it matter to ESA? Quantify the impact if possible.
- What is the current state-of-the-art and its limitations?

## Proposed Approach

[3-4 paragraphs]
- What is the proposed technical solution?
- What methodology will be used?
- What are the key technical steps/work packages?
- What tools, facilities, or data are needed?

## Innovation & Novelty

[1-2 paragraphs]
- What is new about this approach compared to existing solutions?
- What specific advantage does it provide?
- Reference prior art and clearly differentiate.

## ESA Relevance

[1-2 paragraphs]
- Which ESA programme(s) or mission(s) does this support?
- What specific capability does it enable or improve?
- How does it fit in the ESA technology roadmap?

## Technical Readiness

[1 paragraph]
- Current TRL: X
- Target TRL: Y
- Justification for TRL assessment
- Key risks and mitigations

## Expected Outcomes

[1-2 paragraphs]
- Concrete deliverables (reports, prototypes, software, demonstrations)
- Measurable KPIs (performance metrics, accuracy targets, etc.)
- Path to higher TRL after OSIP activity
